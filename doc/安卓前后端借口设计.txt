安卓前后端借口设计

---Register---
Android-->server
	userName:String
	userNName:String
	userTel:String
	userIdentiNum:String(用户身份证号码,与userId不是一个数据)
	password:String
	verifyNum:String(验证码)
response:
	code:[success or failed]
	message[reason]

---Login---
Android-->server
	userTel:String
	password:String
	channel_id:[get from baiduyuntui]
	location:[x,y] (x belong to [-180,180], y belong to [-90,90])
response:
	code:[success or failed]
	message[reason]	
	user_id:
---UpdateLocation---
request:
	user_id:
	location:[x,y] (x belong to [-180,180], y belong to [-90,90])
response:
	code:[success or failed]
	message[reason]	
---Logout---
request:
	user_id:
response:
	code:[success or failed]
	message[reason]
